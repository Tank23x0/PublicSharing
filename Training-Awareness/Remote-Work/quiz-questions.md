# Remote Work Security Quiz

## Question 1
**What WiFi encryption standard should you AVOID using?**

A) WPA3  
B) WPA2  
C) WEP  
D) Both A and B

✅ **Correct Answer: C) WEP**

---

## Question 2
**When should you connect to your company VPN while working from home?**

A) Only when accessing sensitive files  
B) Before accessing any company resources  
C) Only when on public WiFi  
D) VPN is not necessary at home

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Before accessing any company resources**

---

## Question 3
**Is it acceptable to let family members use your work laptop?**

A) Yes, as long as they don't access work files  
B) Yes, but only for educational purposes  
C) No, work devices should be used only for work  
D) Yes, if you create a separate user account

✅ **Correct Answer: C) No, work devices should be used only for work**

---

## Question 4
**You need to work from a coffee shop. What's the safest approach?**

A) Connect directly to the coffee shop WiFi  
B) Use the coffee shop WiFi but avoid sensitive files  
C) Use the coffee shop WiFi with your VPN connected  
D) Ask the barista for the "secure" network

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Use the coffee shop WiFi with your VPN connected**

---

## Question 5
**What should you do with sensitive documents you print at home?**

A) Throw them in the regular trash  
B) Recycle them  
C) Shred them using a cross-cut shredder  
D) Keep them in a pile on your desk

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Shred them using a cross-cut shredder**

---

## Question 6
**How should you position your work monitor at home?**

A) Facing a window for natural light  
B) Away from windows and areas where others might see  
C) It doesn't matter since you're at home  
D) Next to the front door for easy access

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Away from windows and areas where others might see**

---

## Question 7
**Your router still has its default admin password. Is this a security risk?**

A) No, only IT needs to worry about that  
B) No, the WiFi password is what matters  
C) Yes, attackers can easily find default passwords and take control  
D) Maybe, but it's not a big deal

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Yes, attackers can easily find default passwords and take control**

---

## Question 8
**During a video call, you need to share your screen. What should you do first?**

A) Share immediately to save time  
B) Close or minimize windows containing sensitive information  
C) Ask everyone to close their eyes  
D) Screen sharing is always safe

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Close or minimize windows containing sensitive information**

---

## Question 9
**Why might smart speakers (Alexa, Google Home) be a security concern during work calls?**

A) They use too much electricity  
B) They might record or transmit sensitive conversations  
C) They slow down WiFi  
D) They are not a concern at all

✅ **Correct Answer: B) They might record or transmit sensitive conversations**

---

## Question 10
**You receive an email from "IT Support" asking you to install a VPN update by clicking a link. What should you do?**

A) Click the link immediately to stay secure  
B) Forward it to colleagues so they can update too  
C) Contact IT through known channels to verify the request  
D) Reply to ask if it's legitimate

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Contact IT through known channels to verify the request**
