# Social Engineering Quiz

## Question 1
**What is social engineering?**

A) A type of software development methodology  
B) Psychological manipulation to trick people into security mistakes  
C) Engineering solutions for social problems  
D) A network security protocol

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Psychological manipulation to trick people into security mistakes**

---

## Question 2
**Which psychological trigger involves creating a sense of time pressure?**

A) Authority  
B) Social proof  
C) Urgency  
D) Reciprocity

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Urgency**

---

## Question 3
**Someone finds a USB drive in the parking lot labeled "Executive Salaries." What should they do?**

A) Plug it into their computer to identify the owner  
B) Give it to a colleague to check  
C) Turn it in to IT security without plugging it in  
D) Throw it away

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Turn it in to IT security without plugging it in**

---

## Question 4
**What is "pretexting"?**

A) Writing text before a meeting  
B) Creating a fabricated scenario to steal information  
C) Sending preview messages before the main attack  
D) A type of text encryption

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Creating a fabricated scenario to steal information**

---

## Question 5
**Someone in a delivery uniform asks you to hold the door to a secure area. What should you do?**

A) Hold the door - it's polite and they're clearly a delivery person  
B) Politely decline and direct them to reception to get proper access  
C) Ignore them completely  
D) Follow them to see where they go

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Politely decline and direct them to reception to get proper access**

---

## Question 6
**A caller claims to be from IT and says they need your password to fix a security issue. What should you do?**

A) Give them the password to resolve the issue quickly  
B) Ask for their name and call the IT help desk directly to verify  
C) Give them a fake password to test them  
D) Change your password and then give them the old one

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Ask for their name and call the IT help desk directly to verify**

---

## Question 7
**What is "tailgating" in security terms?**

A) Following someone through a secured door without proper credentials  
B) Following someone home from work  
C) Monitoring someone's internet activity  
D) Copying someone's work style

✅ **Correct Answer: A) Following someone through a secured door without proper credentials**

---

## Question 8
**Which of these is a red flag for a social engineering attack?**

A) A request that follows normal procedures  
B) An email from a verified sender you communicate with regularly  
C) Unusual urgency combined with requests to bypass procedures  
D) A meeting scheduled through the official calendar

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Unusual urgency combined with requests to bypass procedures**

---

## Question 9
**Your "CEO" emails asking you to urgently purchase gift cards and send the codes. What is this likely?**

A) A legitimate request from a busy executive  
B) A business email compromise (BEC) / social engineering attack  
C) A team-building exercise  
D) A test of your responsiveness

✅ **Correct Answer: B) A business email compromise (BEC) / social engineering attack**

---

## Question 10
**What percentage of cyberattacks involve some form of social engineering?**

A) 25%  
B) 50%  
C) 75%  
D) 98%

✅ **Correct Answer: D) 98%**
