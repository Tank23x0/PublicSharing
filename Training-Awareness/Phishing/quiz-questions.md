# Phishing Awareness Quiz

## Question 1
**What percentage of cyberattacks begin with a phishing email?**

A) 45%  
B) 67%  
C) 91%  
D) 23%

✅ **Correct Answer: C) 91%**

---

## Question 2
**Which of the following is a red flag indicating a phishing email?**

A) The email uses your full name in the greeting  
B) The email creates a sense of urgency  
C) The email comes from a verified sender  
D) The email has perfect grammar

✅ **Correct Answer: B) The email creates a sense of urgency**

---

## Question 3
**What is "spear phishing"?**

A) Phishing via phone calls  
B) Mass emails sent to millions of people  
C) Targeted phishing using personal information about the victim  
D) Phishing via text messages

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Targeted phishing using personal information about the victim**

---

## Question 4
**What should you do FIRST when you receive a suspicious email?**

A) Forward it to everyone as a warning  
B) Click the link to investigate  
C) Delete it immediately without reporting  
D) Stop and avoid clicking any links or attachments

✅ **Correct Answer: D) Stop and avoid clicking any links or attachments**

---

## Question 5
**You receive an email from "IT Support" asking you to verify your password by clicking a link. What should you do?**

A) Click the link since it's from IT  
B) Reply with your password to save time  
C) Contact IT through official channels to verify the request  
D) Ignore all emails from IT

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Contact IT through official channels to verify the request**

---

## Question 6
**How can you verify where a link in an email actually goes?**

A) Click it and see where it takes you  
B) Trust the displayed text  
C) Hover over the link to preview the actual URL  
D) There's no way to check

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Hover over the link to preview the actual URL**

---

## Question 7
**Which type of phishing specifically targets senior executives?**

A) Spear phishing  
B) Smishing  
C) Whaling  
D) Vishing

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Whaling**

---

## Question 8
**A legitimate company will ask for your password via email.**

A) True  
B) False

✅ **Correct Answer: B) False**

---

## Question 9
**What is "smishing"?**

A) Phishing via social media  
B) Phishing via SMS text messages  
C) Phishing via voice calls  
D) Phishing via video chat

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Phishing via SMS text messages**

---

## Question 10
**After reporting a phishing email, what should you do next?**

A) Keep it in your inbox for reference  
B) Forward it to colleagues  
C) Delete it from your inbox  
D) Reply to the sender

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Delete it from your inbox**
