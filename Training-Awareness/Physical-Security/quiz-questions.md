# Physical Security Quiz

## Question 1
**What is "tailgating" in physical security?**

A) Following a vehicle too closely  
B) Following someone through a secure door without using your own credentials  
C) Staying late at work  
D) Walking behind someone slowly

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Following someone through a secure door without using your own credentials**

---

## Question 2
**Someone you don't recognize asks you to hold the door because "they forgot their badge." What should you do?**

A) Hold the door - it's polite  
B) Tell them to wait and close the door behind you  
C) Politely decline and direct them to reception  
D) Ask for their badge number and let them in

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Politely decline and direct them to reception**

---

## Question 3
**What is a "clean desk policy"?**

A) Wiping down your desk daily  
B) Securing sensitive documents and clearing your workspace when not in use  
C) Only eating clean food at your desk  
D) Having a minimalist desk setup

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Securing sensitive documents and clearing your workspace when not in use**

---

## Question 4
**What keyboard shortcut locks a Windows computer?**

A) Ctrl+Alt+Delete  
B) Alt+F4  
C) Windows+L  
D) Ctrl+Shift+Escape

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Windows+L**

---

## Question 5
**You find a USB drive in the parking lot labeled "Payroll Data." What should you do?**

A) Plug it into your computer to find the owner  
B) Give it to a colleague to check  
C) Turn it in to IT Security without plugging it in  
D) Keep it as a free USB drive

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Turn it in to IT Security without plugging it in**

---

## Question 6
**Your laptop is stolen from your car. When should you report it?**

A) After you've searched everywhere  
B) When you get a replacement  
C) Immediately  
D) Within 30 days

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Immediately**

---

## Question 7
**Which of the following is an acceptable way to handle sensitive paper documents you no longer need?**

A) Regular trash can  
B) Recycling bin  
C) Cross-cut shredder  
D) Tear them in half

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Cross-cut shredder**

---

## Question 8
**Why should visitors be escorted in secure areas?**

A) To be polite and welcoming  
B) To prevent unauthorized access and protect sensitive information  
C) Company policy requires it for insurance purposes  
D) To help them find their way around

✅ **Correct Answer: B) To prevent unauthorized access and protect sensitive information**

---

## Question 9
**You notice someone in your office area you don't recognize and they're not wearing a visitor badge. What should you do?**

A) Assume they're a new employee  
B) Ignore them and continue working  
C) Politely ask if you can help them and verify their authorization  
D) Call the police immediately

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Politely ask if you can help them and verify their authorization**

---

## Question 10
**What is "shoulder surfing"?**

A) A beach activity  
B) Looking over someone's shoulder to see their screen or what they're typing  
C) A type of massage  
D) A swimming technique

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Looking over someone's shoulder to see their screen or what they're typing**
