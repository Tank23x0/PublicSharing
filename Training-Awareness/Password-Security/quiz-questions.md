# Password Security Quiz

## Question 1
**Which factor is MOST important for password strength?**

A) Using special characters (@#$%)  
B) Password length  
C) Using uppercase letters  
D) Changing passwords monthly

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Password length**

---

## Question 2
**Which of these is the STRONGEST password?**

A) P@$$w0rd!  
B) Qwerty123456  
C) MyDogLoves2EatPurpleSocks!  
D) Admin2024

✅ **Correct Answer: C) MyDogLoves2EatPurpleSocks!**

---

## Question 3
**What is Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)?**

A) Using the same password on multiple sites  
B) Changing your password multiple times  
C) Requiring two or more forms of verification to access an account  
D) Having multiple user accounts

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Requiring two or more forms of verification to access an account**

---

## Question 4
**Which MFA method is considered MOST secure?**

A) SMS text codes  
B) Email verification codes  
C) Hardware security keys (like YubiKey)  
D) Security questions

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Hardware security keys (like YubiKey)**

---

## Question 5
**What is the biggest problem with reusing passwords?**

A) It's harder to remember which password goes where  
B) If one account is breached, all accounts using that password are compromised  
C) Password managers won't work correctly  
D) It violates company policy

✅ **Correct Answer: B) If one account is breached, all accounts using that password are compromised**

---

## Question 6
**A password manager is:**

A) A person who manages passwords for your team  
B) Software that generates and securely stores unique passwords  
C) A spreadsheet for tracking passwords  
D) Your browser's built-in password saver

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Software that generates and securely stores unique passwords**

---

## Question 7
**Someone claiming to be from IT calls and asks for your password. What should you do?**

A) Give it to them since IT needs access  
B) Refuse and report the call to your security team  
C) Give them a fake password  
D) Ask them to send an email request instead

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Refuse and report the call to your security team**

---

## Question 8
**What is a "passphrase"?**

A) A password that includes the word "pass"  
B) A sequence of random words used as a password  
C) A password shared between multiple people  
D) The hint you use to remember your password

✅ **Correct Answer: B) A sequence of random words used as a password**

---

## Question 9
**Approximately how long would it take to crack a 16-character passphrase?**

A) A few hours  
B) Several days  
C) A few years  
D) Millions of years

✅ **Correct Answer: D) Millions of years**

---

## Question 10
**Which of these is an acceptable place to store passwords?**

A) A sticky note under your keyboard  
B) A spreadsheet on your desktop  
C) A reputable password manager  
D) Your phone's notes app (unencrypted)

✅ **Correct Answer: C) A reputable password manager**

---

## Bonus Question
**MFA can block what percentage of automated attacks?**

A) 50%  
B) 75%  
C) 90%  
D) 99.9%

✅ **Correct Answer: D) 99.9%**
