# Ransomware Prevention Quiz

## Question 1
**What is ransomware?**

A) Software that demands payment for technical support  
B) Malware that encrypts files and demands payment for decryption  
C) A virus that slows down your computer  
D) Email spam asking for money

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Malware that encrypts files and demands payment for decryption**

---

## Question 2
**What is the #1 delivery method for ransomware?**

A) USB drives  
B) Software vulnerabilities  
C) Phishing emails  
D) Public WiFi networks

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Phishing emails**

---

## Question 3
**What is the "3-2-1 backup rule"?**

A) Back up 3 times a day, 2 times a week, 1 time a month  
B) Keep 3 copies on 2 different media types with 1 copy offsite  
C) Wait 3 seconds, check 2 times, click 1 time  
D) Use 3 passwords, 2 accounts, 1 device

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Keep 3 copies on 2 different media types with 1 copy offsite**

---

## Question 4
**Your computer suddenly shows a message demanding Bitcoin payment to unlock your files. What should you do FIRST?**

A) Pay the ransom to get your files back  
B) Turn off the computer immediately  
C) Disconnect from the network and call IT Security  
D) Try to decrypt the files yourself

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Disconnect from the network and call IT Security**

---

## Question 5
**How often does a ransomware attack occur globally?**

A) Every hour  
B) Every 11 seconds  
C) Every day  
D) Every week

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Every 11 seconds**

---

## Question 6
**What percentage of ransomware victims who pay the ransom get ALL their data back?**

A) 95%  
B) 75%  
C) 50%  
D) 8%

✅ **Correct Answer: D) 8%**

---

## Question 7
**Which of the following is a warning sign of a potential ransomware infection?**

A) Computer running faster than usual  
B) Files that won't open or have strange file extensions  
C) Emails arriving normally  
D) Browser working correctly

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Files that won't open or have strange file extensions**

---

## Question 8
**Why should you NOT turn off a computer suspected of ransomware infection?**

A) It might damage the hardware  
B) The ransom will increase  
C) Forensic evidence may be needed for investigation  
D) The encryption will get worse

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Forensic evidence may be needed for investigation**

---

## Question 9
**Which of the following helps prevent ransomware?**

A) Keeping software updated  
B) Opening email attachments from unknown senders  
C) Disabling antivirus for better performance  
D) Using the same password everywhere

✅ **Correct Answer: A) Keeping software updated**

---

## Question 10
**Why is paying a ransomware demand discouraged?**

A) There's no guarantee you'll get your data back  
B) It encourages more attacks  
C) It may fund criminal organizations  
D) All of the above

✅ **Correct Answer: D) All of the above**
