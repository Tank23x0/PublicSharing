# Compliance Basics Quiz

## Question 1
**What does HIPAA protect?**

A) Credit card data  
B) Protected Health Information (PHI)  
C) European personal data  
D) Cloud service security

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Protected Health Information (PHI)**

---

## Question 2
**What is the maximum GDPR fine for serious violations?**

A) $1 million  
B) $5 million  
C) €20 million or 4% of global annual revenue  
D) $100,000 per incident

✅ **Correct Answer: C) €20 million or 4% of global annual revenue**

---

## Question 3
**PCI-DSS applies to organizations that:**

A) Operate in the European Union  
B) Process, store, or transmit credit card data  
C) Provide healthcare services  
D) Have more than 500 employees

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Process, store, or transmit credit card data**

---

## Question 4
**Under GDPR, how quickly must data breaches be reported to authorities?**

A) Within 24 hours  
B) Within 72 hours  
C) Within 7 days  
D) Within 30 days

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Within 72 hours**

---

## Question 5
**What is the "minimum necessary" principle in HIPAA?**

A) Keep patient records as short as possible  
B) Only access the PHI needed for your specific job function  
C) Use the minimum amount of encryption  
D) Keep the minimum number of employees

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Only access the PHI needed for your specific job function**

---

## Question 6
**SOC 2 compliance demonstrates an organization's:**

A) Financial stability  
B) Security controls and practices to customers  
C) Healthcare data protection  
D) Payment processing capabilities

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Security controls and practices to customers**

---

## Question 7
**Under PCI-DSS, which of the following should NEVER be stored?**

A) Customer names  
B) Transaction dates  
C) CVV/CVC security codes  
D) Last four digits of card numbers

✅ **Correct Answer: C) CVV/CVC security codes**

---

## Question 8
**What should you do if you suspect a compliance violation?**

A) Try to fix it yourself to avoid getting in trouble  
B) Ignore it if it seems minor  
C) Report it immediately to your manager or compliance team  
D) Wait until the next audit

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Report it immediately to your manager or compliance team**

---

## Question 9
**GDPR applies to:**

A) Only companies headquartered in the EU  
B) Only EU citizens  
C) Any organization handling personal data of EU residents  
D) Only healthcare organizations

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Any organization handling personal data of EU residents**

---

## Question 10
**Why is compliance important beyond avoiding fines?**

A) It creates more paperwork  
B) It protects customers, patients, and partners from harm  
C) It's only important for publicly traded companies  
D) It's just a checkbox exercise

✅ **Correct Answer: B) It protects customers, patients, and partners from harm**

---

## Bonus Question
**What does the "right to be forgotten" refer to in GDPR?**

A) Organizations can delete customer records whenever they want  
B) Individuals can request deletion of their personal data  
C) Data must be automatically deleted after one year  
D) Employees must forget customer data when they leave

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Individuals can request deletion of their personal data**
