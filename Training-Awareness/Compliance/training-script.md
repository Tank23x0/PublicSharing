# Security Compliance Basics Training Script

> **Duration:** ~5 minutes | **Target Audience:** All employees

---

## [INTRO - 0:00-0:30]

**[NARRATOR]**

Compliance isn't just about checking boxes—it's about protecting people. When we follow regulations like HIPAA, PCI-DSS, GDPR, and SOC 2, we're protecting customer data, patient privacy, financial security, and personal information.

Let's understand the key regulations and what they mean for your daily work.

---

## [SECTION 1: Why Compliance Matters - 0:30-1:15]

**[NARRATOR]**

Compliance failures have serious consequences.

**[ON SCREEN: Consequences visualization]**

**Financial Penalties**
- GDPR: Up to €20 million or 4% of global revenue
- HIPAA: Up to $1.5 million per violation category per year
- PCI-DSS: $5,000 to $100,000 per month until compliant

**Beyond Fines**
- Loss of customer trust
- Business relationship termination
- Personal liability for executives
- Mandatory public breach notifications
- Lawsuits and class actions

Compliance isn't optional—it's essential for business survival.

---

## [SECTION 2: Key Regulations Overview - 1:15-3:00]

**[NARRATOR]**

Let's briefly cover the major regulations you need to know.

**[ON SCREEN: HIPAA section]**

**HIPAA - Healthcare Data**
Protects health information in the United States.

**Key Requirements:**
- Protect patient health information (PHI)
- Only access PHI when necessary for your job
- Never discuss patient information in public areas
- Report any suspected breaches immediately

**[ON SCREEN: PCI-DSS section]**

**PCI-DSS - Payment Card Data**
Protects credit card information worldwide.

**Key Requirements:**
- Never write down full card numbers
- Don't store card data on local computers
- Use only approved systems for payment processing
- Report suspicious payment activity

**[ON SCREEN: GDPR section]**

**GDPR - Personal Data in EU**
Protects personal data of EU residents.

**Key Requirements:**
- Get consent before collecting personal data
- Only collect data you actually need
- Honor requests to delete or export personal data
- Report breaches within 72 hours

**[ON SCREEN: SOC 2 section]**

**SOC 2 - Service Organization Controls**
Demonstrates security practices to clients.

**Key Requirements:**
- Follow documented security procedures
- Maintain access controls
- Participate in security training
- Report security incidents

---

## [SECTION 3: Compliance in Daily Work - 3:00-4:00]

**[NARRATOR]**

Compliance comes down to everyday actions.

**[ON SCREEN: Daily practices checklist]**

**Access Controls**
- Only access data you need for your job
- Never share your login credentials
- Log off when leaving your workstation

**Data Handling**
- Know what type of data you're handling
- Use approved storage and transmission methods
- Dispose of data properly (shred, secure delete)

**Documentation**
- Follow documented procedures
- Complete required training on time
- Keep accurate records of your activities

**Incident Reporting**
- Report any suspected violations immediately
- Don't try to fix compliance issues yourself
- Cooperate with audits and investigations

---

## [SECTION 4: Your Role in Compliance - 4:00-4:30]

**[NARRATOR]**

Compliance is a team effort. Here's your part:

**[ON SCREEN: Individual responsibilities]**

**You are responsible for:**
- Understanding which regulations apply to your work
- Following established procedures
- Completing required training
- Reporting concerns or violations
- Asking questions when unsure

**When in doubt:**
- Ask your manager
- Contact the compliance team
- Check the policy documentation
- Report rather than guess

---

**[NARRATOR]**

Compliance protects real people—patients, customers, and their families. When you follow these regulations, you're not just avoiding fines—you're honoring the trust people place in our organization.

Stay informed. Follow procedures. Report concerns. And remember: when you're compliant, you're protecting people.
