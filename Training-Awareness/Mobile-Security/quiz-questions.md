# Mobile Device Security Quiz

## Question 1
**What is the minimum recommended PIN length for your smartphone?**

A) 4 digits  
B) 6 digits  
C) 8 digits  
D) PIN is not necessary if you have biometrics

✅ **Correct Answer: B) 6 digits**

---

## Question 2
**What is "smishing"?**

A) A type of malware  
B) Phishing attacks via SMS text messages  
C) Stealing smartphones  
D) Hacking via smart home devices

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Phishing attacks via SMS text messages**

---

## Question 3
**Where should you download apps for your smartphone?**

A) Any website that offers them for free  
B) Only from official app stores (App Store, Google Play)  
C) From email attachments  
D) From QR codes in advertisements

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Only from official app stores (App Store, Google Play)**

---

## Question 4
**A flashlight app asks for permission to access your contacts, microphone, and location. This is:**

A) Normal - apps need various permissions  
B) Suspicious - these permissions aren't needed for a flashlight  
C) Required for the app to work  
D) A sign of a high-quality app

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Suspicious - these permissions aren't needed for a flashlight**

---

## Question 5
**What is "juice jacking"?**

A) Stealing phone batteries  
B) Data theft through public USB charging stations  
C) Draining phone batteries remotely  
D) A type of mobile malware

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Data theft through public USB charging stations**

---

## Question 6
**Your phone is stolen. What should you do FIRST?**

A) Wait to see if someone returns it  
B) Try to locate it using Find My Device  
C) Buy a new phone  
D) Post about it on social media

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Try to locate it using Find My Device**

---

## Question 7
**When using public WiFi on your phone, you should:**

A) Connect freely since phones are secure  
B) Connect only if the network has a password  
C) Use a VPN for all work-related activities  
D) Turn off your phone to avoid hackers

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Use a VPN for all work-related activities**

---

## Question 8
**What does "jailbreaking" or "rooting" a phone do to its security?**

A) Makes it more secure by removing restrictions  
B) Significantly weakens security and violates most work policies  
C) Has no effect on security  
D) Enables better antivirus protection

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Significantly weakens security and violates most work policies**

---

## Question 9
**How long should your phone's auto-lock timer be set for?**

A) 5 minutes  
B) 10 minutes  
C) 1-2 minutes maximum  
D) 30 minutes

✅ **Correct Answer: C) 1-2 minutes maximum**

---

## Question 10
**You receive a text message claiming to be from your bank asking you to click a link to verify your account. What should you do?**

A) Click the link immediately to protect your account  
B) Reply with your account information  
C) Delete the message and contact your bank directly through their official app or website  
D) Forward it to friends to warn them

✅ **Correct Answer: C) Delete the message and contact your bank directly through their official app or website**

---

## Bonus Question
**What should you do with Bluetooth when not actively using it?**

A) Leave it on for convenience  
B) Turn it off to reduce attack surface  
C) It doesn't matter  
D) Put it in "discoverable" mode

✅ **Correct Answer: B) Turn it off to reduce attack surface**
